1 year olds

Parent's Morning Out is our program, guided by qualified staff, that provides a time of growing and learning experiences for children while parents have a few hours to relax and enrich their lives. These classes meet from 8:45am-11:45pm. The ONE YEAR OLD class meets on Tuesdays and Wednesdays. Children can be enrolled for either one or both days. The class size is 8 children with a staff/child ratio of 1:4.

3 year olds

Our THREE YEAR OLD year old program begins to provide structure in the classroom with centers and group activities that provide opportunities for the children to explore his/her world through weekly themes and developmentally appropriate activities. A major part of a three year old child's development is learning social skills and establishing social relationships. There are four classes that meet from 9am-12pm. Two classes meet Monday / Wednesday / Friday with a maximum class size of 14 with a staff/child of 1:7. Our Tuesday / Thursday class size maximum is 12 with a staff/child ration of 1:6.
