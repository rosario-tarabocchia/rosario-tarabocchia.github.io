Weekday Early Education (WEE Care), the preschool program at Wake Forest Baptist Church is an outreach program designed to provide a safe, loving and Christian environment that teaches vaulable social skills and encourages individual independence for preschool-aged children. We meet from 9:00am-12:00pm weekdays during the months of September through May in the Education Building.
