meet our staff

laura wolfe

Laura Wolfe, is the Director of the WEE Preschool and most recently served as Program Assistant for the WEE Preschool since 2004. She is a graduate of Chowan University and East Carolina University. She is married to Tom and is the mother of three, all of whom went through the WEE Preschool. Laura has been a member of Wake Forest Baptist Church for over 15 years.

kennie schwier

Kennie Schwier is the Program Assistant for the WEE Preschool. She previously worked as an assistant in the 2s and 3s. She is from the Wake Forest area and a graduate of Appalachian State University. She and her husband Dave have two boys, Jacob and Brady, who attended WEE Preschool. Kennie enjoys spending time with her family and is looking forward to an exciting school year!
